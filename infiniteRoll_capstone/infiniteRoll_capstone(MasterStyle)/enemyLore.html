Minor Enemies

Bandit

Just a common bandit. A ruffian like this only wants one thing, gold. Also possibly to have been loved more as a child, but we aren't here to get into his issues.

Count Spatula

A spatula that is also part vampire? Well this is bizzare. Count spatula was once a normal spatula that was enchanted to come to life one day, but he had a terrible encounter with a vampire one faithful night. And so he was cursed, or so the story goes...

Ent

Ents are very mysterious creatures to say the least. They are one with nature, and only appear in areas that have fallen out of balance. What could bring them here though? What power could cause so many ents to converge and be so aggresive?

Goblin Grunt

Gasp! What's that creeping the bushes? Oh, its just a goblin. Goblins are common enemies that serve under the ruthless Goblin King. Not much can be said about goblins other then they like shiny objects and they sometimes need a good kick in the rear.

The Goblin King

The ruthless king of all goblins, the Goblin King will stop at nothing to destroy everything in it's path. While the Goblin King is a very dangerous entity, even he fears what lies beyond his kingdom... or possibly even in his kingdom?

Ice Fox

A fearsome yet beautiful creature to behold. This creature has some strange abilities, too! It enjoys running or taking walks on top of lakes and rivers, but he never falls in. This is because once its foot comes in contact with the surface of the water, the water instantly freezes. Allowing the fox to safely travel across any body of water.

Wolf

The wolf may seem pretty vanilla compared to other beasts that lurk in the woods, but it is very dangerous still. Usually wolves hunt in packs, but for some reason they only appear solo around here. Maybe something even more powerful has caused their numbers to shrink?



Major Characters

Chester

Wow! A mimic merchant?! That is exactly what Chester is. While most mimics tend to trick adventurers into their doom, Chester has found that he gets much more from working with adventurers, instead of eating them. You may encouter this merchant lots of times during your adventures, maybe you could stop and tell him stories of magical oubliettes and dangerous beasts.

Steve

Steve, a powerful and ancient entity. No one has ever seen Steve in person, and lived to tell the tale that is. Some folks have claimed that he has left them gifts, telling them that he will fight them once they have gained more power.
