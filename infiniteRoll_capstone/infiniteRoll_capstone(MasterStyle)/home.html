Infinite Roll


Welcome to the world of Infinite Roll! This is a game designed to test the limits of how far you an get on your own in the world of Dungeons and Dragons. You will construct a character based on some presets, and we will roll for all of your stats. This land maybe unforgiving, but you could have the makings of a true hero! Do you think you're up to the challenge? Lets begin.
