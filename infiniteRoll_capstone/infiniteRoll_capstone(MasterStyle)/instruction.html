How To Play


This game is very simple!
Starting with the game page, you will have 3 buttons that you will use, open player stats, open player inventory, and roll. Roll if your most important button, it helps you progress in the game and dictates all events that will be happening.

Second, Open Player Stats, when clicked, will be present you with a menu that displays all of your previously generated stats before the game has started.

Lastly, the Open Player Inventory, when clicked, will present you with what you currently have with you. Such as gold, Magic Items, Pieces of Eight, and Monster parts that you will be able to use on your quest.
